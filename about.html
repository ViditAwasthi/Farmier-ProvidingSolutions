About Us!



This Project


This is a Web Application Made to Resolve the problems Faced by Farmers and Suppliers. By this application we wish to solve the problem of the farmers by providing them Best solution to their Crop Problems and Diseases. Also by this application we Wish to Minimise the gap between the farmer and the supplier by providing them with the feature of the Selling and Buying the crops from anywhere therefore reducing the involvement of the middle man. By this application a Farmer can Directly contact the Supplier and put in a Sell Request, While on the other hand the Supplier comes to know about all the requests made by the farmer for selling his crops. This way we have reduced the involvement of thr midlle man. Thus Providing Great Benefits to both the farmers and the suppliers.
